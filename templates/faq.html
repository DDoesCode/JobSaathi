FAQs

1. How do I search for jobs?

Simply enter your skills in the search box on the home page and click "Search Jobs" to view a list of relevant job openings.

3. How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" link in the navigation bar, fill in your details, and submit the form to create an account.

4. How do I apply for a job?

Once you find a job listing, click the "View Job" link, which will take you to the job listing page on the original site where you can apply.

5. I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
