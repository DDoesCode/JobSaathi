About JobLo

Our Mission

Job Aggregator is designed to make job hunting easier by collecting job listings from multiple popular websites and displaying them in one place. Enter your skills to quickly find jobs suited to your expertise from sites like Internshala, We Work Remotely, Jobspresso, and Remote OK.

How It Works

Simply enter your skills on the search page, and the site will fetch relevant job listings from various platforms. We then display up to 10 job listings that match your skillset, complete with links to apply directly on each platform.
